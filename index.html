Aloha, welcome!

I work with Prof. Giuseppe Torri in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences.

My Ph.D. work focuses on understanding convective organization and propagation associated with the Madden-Julian Oscillation (MJO), especially the physical mechanisms of cold pool dynamics and gravity waves.

My work combines Large Eddy Simulations/Cloud Resolving Models, a Lagrangian Particle Dispersion Model, and Convection-Permitting Models to understand dynamic and thermodynamic cloud processes.
